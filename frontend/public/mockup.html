💬 Value Discovery - Conversation Styles

Option A: Progressive Reveal
One question at a time. Answer unlocks the next question.
How do you currently track IP addresses?
✓ Spreadsheets and manual tracking
Have you experienced IP conflicts?
✓ Yes, frequently causing outages
What's the business impact when conflicts occur?
🔒 Next: Resolution time & effort
Option B: Chat Interface
Natural conversation flow like messaging. Questions and answers as bubbles.
👋 Let's understand your IP management challenges. How do you currently track IP addresses across your organization?
We use spreadsheets mostly. Some teams have their own tracking methods.
Spreadsheets can get messy! Have you experienced any IP conflicts or addressing issues?
Yes, we had 3 major outages last quarter from duplicate IPs.
That sounds painful. What was the business impact of those outages?
Option C: Branching Conversation
Questions adapt based on your answers. Different paths for different situations.
Have you experienced IP conflicts? Yes
How often do conflicts occur? Weekly
What's the typical resolution time?
🔒 Impact on business operations
If "No" → Skip to: Proactive planning questions
